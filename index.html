Monty Oum

Influential Animator and DDR Master

Monty Oum was a self-taught animator who worked at Rooster Teeth, making animations for Red Vs. Blue. Afterwards, he created the animated series RWBY. He was occasionally on the RoosterTeeth podcasts and RTLife videos. Visit his RTWikia for more information.

His Influence

Monty created many works of art, proving to be an exceptional animator and producer. RWBY is loved by fans across the world with many cosplayers crafting new costumes every day as well as avid viewers who patiently wait for the next volume. Monty's work with Red Vs. Blue paved the path for future seasons and episodes.

Pushing, Working Harder

Every project, Monty would work hours and hours, sometimes without sleep to accomplish what he wanted to make. His life was centered around his work and due to this, he was able to achieve amazing detail in the character designs and the combat scenes that he created. His co-workers at RoosterTeeth remember him most for being the one who pushes the team to get last minute scenes into Red Vs. Blue. Monty always strived for the best, never tolerating any less.

"It's easy to forget to sleep when you're working on something cool, so you just work as hard as you can and still, there is never enough time... We tend to be masters of our own fate; the only thing that stops us from doing [what we want to do] is time."

Monty Oum

Monty Oum suffered a severe allergic reaction during a medical procedure and fell into a coma on January 22, 2015.

He died on February 1, 2015.
